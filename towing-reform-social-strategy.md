# Towing Reform Social Media Strategy — 2026 NYS Session

## Target Bills
| Bill | Title | Key Provision |
|------|-------|---------------|
| S7717 | Statewide Fee Cap | Caps non-consensual tow fees at $125 |
| S4793 | Drop Fee / Wreck Chaser Reform | Mandates drop fees; criminalizes ignoring them |
| A8858 | Assembly Companion | Assembly-side towing consumer protections |

## 2. Content Pillars

### Pillar A — "The Receipt Drop" (TikTok / Instagram Reels)
- Format: Green screen with real towing receipt
- Hook: "This tow cost $450 in Buffalo. If S7717 passes, the law would cap it at $125. Why is Albany waiting?"
- Goal: Viral awareness — make the price gap impossible to ignore

### Pillar B — "The Drop Fee" Social Experiment (Reels)
- Format: 30-second explainer
- Hook: "They can't take your car if you're standing right there. It's called a 'Drop Fee.' S4793 makes this a crime to ignore."
- Goal: Educate drivers on existing rights and the gap S4793 fills

### Pillar C — "Shadow Board" (X / Twitter)
- Format: Quote-tweet or tag committee chairs whenever a towing scam hits the news
- Hook: "Hey @NilyRozic, another New Yorker just got scammed for $600. When are we getting a vote on A8858?"
- Goal: Sustained public pressure — tie real stories to legislative inaction

## 3. Four-Week "Splatter" Calendar — March 2026

### Week 1: Economic Justice
- **Platform:** Local community Facebook groups
- **Message:** "Predatory towing is a tax on the working class. $500 is a week's rent. Support S7717 for a $125 cap."
- **Assets:** Receipt Drop TikTok, infographic comparing tow fees vs. weekly expenses

### Week 2: Public Safety
- **Platform:** Nextdoor, local Crime Watch groups
- **Message:** "S4793 stops 'wreck chasers' from racing to accidents and creating more crashes. Support safer streets."
- **Assets:** Drop Fee explainer reel, news clips of wreck-chaser incidents

### Week 3: Small Business
- **Platform:** LinkedIn, outreach to Business Improvement Districts (BIDs)
- **Message:** "Predatory towing at malls drives customers to Amazon. Protect our local shops with fair towing laws."
- **Assets:** LinkedIn article, BID email template

### Week 4: Final Push
- **Platform:** X / Twitter (mass-tag thread)
- **Message:** "The session is halfway over. Will you protect drivers or predatory towers? Vote Yea on S7717."
- **Assets:** Thread tagging all 150 Assembly members, screenshot of YEA vote as social proof
