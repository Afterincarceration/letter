Restorative Reentry Circle Sign-Up Sheet

Thank you for your interest in joining the Restorative Reentry Circle hosted by After Incarceration. This space is designed to support formerly incarcerated community members in Newburgh through a guided process of healing, reflection, and reconnection.

Each circle includes four sessions held during May–June 2025. Participants who complete all four sessions will receive a stipend of $100 per session.
